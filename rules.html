Please , Read and follow rules of NonameVM

If you don't follow the rules, expect a ban!

R1. Don't break the law. Do not use NonameVM or NonameVM's network to violate United Kingdom and Russian Federation government law, or international law. If NonameVM becomes aware a crime has been committed through its service, you will be immediately banned, and your activities may be reported to the authorities if necessary.

COPPA is also enforced, please do not use NonameVM if you are under the age of 13 years old.
R2. No running DoS/DDoS tools. Do not use NonameVM to DoS/DDoS an indivdiual, business, company, or anyone else.
R3. No spam distribution. Do not spam any emails using this service or push spam in general.
R4. Do not abuse any exploits. Do not abuse any exploits, additionally if you see someone abusing exploits or you need to report one, please contact me on the Discord server: here
R5. Do not impersonate other users. Do not impersonate other members of NonameVM/CollabVM. If caught, you'll be temporarily disconnected, and banned if necessary.
R6. One vote per person. Do not use any methods or tools to bypass the vote restriction. Only one vote per person is allowed, no matter what. Anybody who is caught doing this will be banned.
R7. Do not use Remote Administration Tools. Do not use any remote administration tools (ex: DarkComet, NanoCore, AnyDesk, TeamViewer, Orcus, etc.)

This includes posting your remote connections IDs on the NonameVM chat
R8. Do not bypass xNet. Do not attempt to bypass the blocking provided by xNet, especially if it is being used to break Rule 1, Rule 2, or Rule 7 (or run trojans , forkie and other malware things).
R9. Do not perform destructive actions constantly. Any user may not destroy the VM (rendering it unusable constantly), install/reinstall the operating system (except on VM5 or VM6), or run bots that do such. This includes bots that spam massive amounts of keyboard/mouse input ("kitting").
R10. Do not cryptomine. Attempting to mine cryptocurrency on the VMs will result in a kick, and then a permanent ban if you keep attempting. Besides, it's not like you're gonna make any money off it.
R11. Do not make the VM slow on purpose. This includes spam copy and pasting on the system, opening too many tabs, forkie bombs , opening every program possible, using software such as NotMyFault and ending critical processes.
R12. NSFW Warning Please note that NSFW content is allowed on our anarchy VM (VM0b0t), and is viewed regularly. In addition, while we give a good effort to keep NSFW off the main VMs, people will occasionally slip it through.

What you will NOT be banned for.
You have the right to freedom here and you can download whatever you want without breaking the above mentioned rules. For spamming in the chat and insulting users you will get mute, but for insulting the administration if necessary we will ban you.

What should I do if I see someone breaking the rules?
It's very simple, ping the role @Urgent Staff on our discord server and write Username of the violator and what he does, it will be better if you attach a proof, otherwise write us to the mail

Warning !
These rules are likely to be modified (rules added, modified or outright deleted), so please keep an eye out regularly.
Mods/Admins of NonameVM will always make the final decision of banning, you will not persuade us to unban your friend because of something they did.
